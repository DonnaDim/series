Chapter 1 Welcome

This book is a lesson series that will introduce you to open practices and data science. It is under heavy development.

It is modeled after Mozilla’s Open Leadership Training Series.

Openscapes is run by Julia Stewart Lowndes, a Mozilla Fellow and marine scientist at the National Center for Ecological Analysis & Synthesis (NCEAS). Learn more at openscapes.org.
